These tunes are provided for the use of our members. If you would like to use them on your own website, please contact us. Full tune list and drummers' scores coming soon. BWW are files meant to be read using Bagpipe Music Writer or Bagpipe Player software or similar.

Tune list abbreviations:

  • A. Apprentice Tunes: These tunes should be learned during a piper's or drummer's apprentice period with the band.
  • J. Journeyman Tunes: These tunes are for those who have played with the band for a while, and are ready to learn more.
  • M. Master Tunes: These tunes are for those who have played with the band for a long time. They will be more difficult to learn.
  • X. Extraordinary Tunes: These tunes are extra tunes that we rarely play, but which might provide a chance to hone your skills.

Common tune sets listed after the tunes list.
